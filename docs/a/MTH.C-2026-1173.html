Statement of record: `Eidometry.Thesis.forcing_theorem_measurable` (Eidometry thesis, section Forcing).

Over an arbitrary measurable state space with an arbitrary measure-valued family of dynamics, the bisimilarity relation for a target family is itself a valid spec-respecting equivalence, is coarser than every other such equivalence, and every encoder that satisfies the specification has a kernel at least as fine as it. No countability, finiteness, or topology is assumed.

Discharged by [MTH.R-2026-1173].
