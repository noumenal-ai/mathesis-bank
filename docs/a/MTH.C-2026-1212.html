Statement of record: `Eidometry.Thesis.counterfactual_cfread_unreadable` (Eidometry thesis, section Counterfactuals).

A counterfactual quantity cannot be recovered from interventional data alone: two distributions over outcomes can agree on every intervention result yet still disagree on the counterfactual, so the counterfactual is not a function of what interventions reveal. This gap — Pearl's third causal rung sitting above the second — is why the two levels must be kept distinct rather than collapsed into one.
