Deposit

The bank accepts deposits the way arXiv accepts papers and GenBank accepts sequences: you submit, an automated gate checks it, and it enters the public record. Here the gate is a proof kernel, and it re-derives every deposit from scratch — it never takes the deposit's word for what it proves.

A deposit is a pull request.

The gate re-derives it in CI. No human judges the mathematics.

A maintainer merge signs off only the plain-language description.

What you can deposit

  • A result — a statement together with a machine-checked proof.
  • A definition — a formal notion others can build on (the Dictionary).
  • A claim — a statement of record, open for a result to discharge.

How to deposit

  1. Add a folder to the repository with three files: 
    deposits/<your-slug>/
  submission.lean   the Lean source the gate builds and re-derives
  deposit.toml      declaration names, toolchain pin, the claim it discharges (if any)
  gloss.md          your plain-language description (author-provided, not machine-checked)
  2. Open a pull request.
  3. The gate runs automatically and posts its verdict. Fix and push until it is green.
  4. A maintainer confirms the description reads faithfully to the formal statement and merges. The deposit is assigned an accession number and enters the public record.

What the gate checks

Every check below is re-run from the deposit's frozen proof export. None is read from what the manifest claims about itself.

  • Kernel replay — the proof is accepted by the Lean 4 kernel.
  • Axiom closure — it depends only on propext Classical.choice Quot.sound. Anything more must be declared, and is flagged for review rather than admitted silently.
  • Definition audit — a definition's body is held to the same axiom whitelist, so a definition cannot smuggle in an assumption.
  • Statement identity — a result that discharges a claim must prove that exact claim, closed over every definition it names.
  • Non-triviality — a statement that is syntactically trivial, or a definition that is vacuously true, is routed to human review rather than published as a result.

What the gate does not check: that the plain-language description matches the formal statement. That is author-provided and confirmed only by the maintainer's sign-off. See the Charter.

Status

The founding volume was deposited by the maintainers, and the gate above runs live on every change to the bank. Deposits from outside the maintainer team are being enabled after a final security review of the untrusted-build path; until then, open an issue on the repository to propose one.
