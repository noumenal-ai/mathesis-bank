When the candidate structures are pseudometrics instead of partitions, the structure forced by the metric specification equals the Ferns least fixed point (the standard bisimulation metric). The same construction, read in the order dual, extends the result from the partition setting to the metric setting.

**Original contribution.** The corpus lemma equates the metric-specification forced object with the known bisimulation-metric fixed point; the thesis presents it as showing the forcing principle survives the shift from partitions to pseudometrics, obtained by reading the same construction in the order dual.
