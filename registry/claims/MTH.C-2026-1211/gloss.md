Statement of record: `Eidometry.Thesis.counterfactual_ct3a_discharged` (Eidometry thesis, section Counterfactuals).

One of a pair of smoothness conjectures: the other form was disproved, but this one holds. It states that for any measurable state space equipped with a countable family of measurable readout functions and a measurable transition, one can construct a countable family of measurable sets that respects the behavioral equivalence (bisimilarity) of states and separates its equivalence classes — with no topological-regularity assumption on the space.

Discharged by [MTH.R-2026-1211].
