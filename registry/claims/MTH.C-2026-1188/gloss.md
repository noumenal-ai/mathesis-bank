Statement of record: `Eidometry.Thesis.classical_conserved_geometry` (Eidometry thesis, section Experiments).

When the dynamics conserve every target quantity, the indistinguishability relation collapses to exactly the partition induced by those quantities; the state geometry of a conservative system is just the target partition. Companion result: a harmonic oscillator's conserved energy yields no readout of position.
