Statement of record: `Eidometry.Thesis.quantum_moment_cone` (Eidometry thesis, section Experiments).

For a single qubit evolving under its own unitary dynamics, the coarsest distinction between states that full tomographic measurement can support is exactly the range of the moment map. Only the measurement setup is supplied; the resulting state geometry is proved equal to this independently defined object.
