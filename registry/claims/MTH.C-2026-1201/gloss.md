Statement of record: `Eidometry.Thesis.dyadic_base` (Eidometry thesis, section Executed runs).

Every finite executed IEEE-754 float32 value, read as a real number, is exactly a dyadic rational (an integer divided by a power of two). This is the bridge that makes executed floating-point arithmetic exact mathematics rather than an approximation.
