Statement of record: `Eidometry.Thesis.counterfactual_ct3b_refuter` (Eidometry thesis, section Counterfactuals).

There exist measurable read functions and a measurable set whose closure under 'reads the same' (its saturation) is not measurable — an explicit classical analytic, non-Borel counterexample. This refutes, as stated, the conjecture that every bisimilarity on a standard Borel space admits a Borel selector.
