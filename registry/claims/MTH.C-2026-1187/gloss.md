Statement of record: `Eidometry.Thesis.quantum_wigner_gap` (Eidometry thesis, section Experiments).

On a three-level system there exist two states that stay indistinguishable for all time under both the tridiagonal measurement family and the position observable, yet are not related by any additive, real-scaling, modulus-preserving, population-preserving map. Below full tomographic measurement, dynamical indistinguishability is strictly coarser than symmetry equivalence.
